About

I will be available for new work from October 2020.

If you'd like to take a look at some of my work visit the case studies page. I also have design work on Dribbble and code work on CodePen.

Hello! I'm a contract Interface Designer and Front-end Developer, based in England. I have over ten years experience working in different roles across the web industry from User Research through to Design and Development.

In summer 2019 I made the decision to step out on my own and start Witts Design and Development Limited. I now work with companies that I feel passionate about. Most recently I was working with the Foods Standards Agency as an Interaction Designer, designing and researching the systems that get food from farm to fork.

Feel free to say “hello”. Alternatively, shift the paradigm and ping me on LinkedIn where we can think outside the box in order to circle back to results driven blue sky thinking.

Colophon

This website is version 2 of v2017. It was designed and developed in September 2020. The tools used to create it were Adobe XD and Illustrator and Visual Studio Code. It uses one typeface, Woodford Borne, in it's various weights.

Version 2 builds on version 1 with a nearly complete rewrite of the CSS (leading to a saving of 12.7 kilobytes). It introduced a case studies landing page and the copy on case studies has been drastically cut down in favour of images. It has been developed with HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

The layout is based on a 12 column grid system and a fairly extensive colour palette.
